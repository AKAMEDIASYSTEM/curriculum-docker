Curriculum

Welcome!

Curriculum is a tool for small, creative groups - about 4 to 8 people is the best size. It works by accumulating fragments of your group's HTTP browsing (Wikipedia, Stack Overflow, etc) and making them into a stream that only your group can see.

  • It's a group feed. No one person can be traced back to any particular term you see.
  • It's HTTP-only, no HTTPS. That means it doesn't look at Facebook, Twitter, banking sites, most shopping sites, etc.
  • It's ephemeral—everything is permanently deleted after 28 days. Curriculum is about a stream of ideas you and your colleagues are encountering, it's not designed for any kind of analysis.
  • It's open-source. You can read every line of the code and make sure it's doing what you want.
